Online farming route system (OFRS)

Online Farming Route System is a web-based application designed to connect farmers and buyers through an optimized transportation and route-planning process. The system allows farmers to list their crops with location details and enables buyers to find nearby produce easily. By using distance-based route logic, the application suggests efficient delivery paths, helping reduce transportation cost, delivery time, and post-harvest losses. This project focuses on improving agricultural supply-chain efficiency and providing farmers with better market access through a simple and user-friendly digital platform.
